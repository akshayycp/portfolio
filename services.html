Process

How a project moves

Every job — design or motion — runs through the same three stages, borrowed straight from how a film gets made.

01
Pre-Production

Brief & plan

Discovery call, references and moodboard. For motion work, this is also where the script or storyboard gets locked.

02
Production

Design & build

Layouts, screens or animation get built out in full — with a check-in round before anything is treated as final.

03
Post-Production

Finish & deliver

Color grading, sound and timing pass, then export in every format you'll actually need — print, web or reel.
